Navigate through a dangerous dungeon filled with mathematical challenges. Solve puzzles, defeat monsters, and collect treasures to achieve the highest score possible!

Attack (+): Calculated precision strike - moderate damage with consistency
Slash (-): Mathematical fury - slightly higher damage variance
Power (*): Multiplication attack - high damage potential with risk
Pierce (/): Division precision - reliable damage with good accuracy
Defend: Reduce incoming damage by half for one turn

Player Stats

  • Health: Your life force. Reaches 0 = Game Over. Restored by potions and level ups.
  • Score: Points earned from defeating monsters, solving puzzles, and finding treasures.
  • Level: Increases every 100 points. Higher levels = more damage and health.
  • Potions: Restore 30-50 health points. Use wisely!

Math Challenges

Puzzles include:

  • Basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
  • Modular arithmetic (remainder operations)
  • Difficulty scales with your progress
  • Wrong answers cause 5 HP damage
  • Skip option available for 10 HP cost

Strategy Tips

  • Explore systematically - visited rooms are marked and safe to return to
  • Manage your health carefully - use potions before critical battles
  • Level up by earning points - each level increases your combat effectiveness
  • Different combat actions have different damage ranges - experiment!
  • Defending can be crucial against strong monsters
  • Take time to solve puzzles correctly - the health penalty adds up

Winning Conditions

There's no traditional "win" condition - the goal is to achieve the highest score possible before your health reaches zero. Challenge yourself to:

  • Reach higher levels through consistent point earning
  • Explore more of the dungeon safely
  • Master the combat system for efficient monster defeats
  • Solve puzzles quickly and accurately
